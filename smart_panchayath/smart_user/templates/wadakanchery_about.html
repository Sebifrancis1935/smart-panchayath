Wadakanchery Municipality is a vibrant and growing urban local body located in Thrissur district of Kerala state. It is one of the 41 wards in Thrissur district, and is home to a diverse population. The municipality is known for its rich cultural heritage and historical significance, with several ancient temples and monuments located within its boundaries. Wadakanchery is also an important commercial hub, with several textile mills and power loom industries operating in the area. The municipality is well-connected to other parts of the state and country through a network of roads and railways, making it an attractive destination for businesses and industries. Wadakanchery is also home to a number of educational institutions, including schools and colleges, providing quality education to the local population. The municipality is administered by an elected council, which is responsible for providing various civic amenities and services to the residents.
